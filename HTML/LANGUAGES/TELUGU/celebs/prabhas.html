Prabhas

Indian Actor

Uppalapati Venkata Suryanarayana Prabhas Raju (born 23 October 1979) is an Indian actor who predominantly works in Telugu language films. One of the highest-paid actors in Indian cinema, Prabhas has featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list since 2015 and has received seven Filmfare Awards nominations, a Nandi Award, and a SIIMA Award. In 2015, Prabhas portrayed the title role in S. S. Rajamouli's action film Baahubali: The Beginning and reprised his role in its sequel, Baahubali 2: The Conclusion (2017).
